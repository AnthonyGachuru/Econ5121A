Lecture Notes

This repository hosts an open-source writing project of lecture notes for the first-year gradate-level econometrics. They are companions of the my in-class teaching, but not substitute for lecture taking.

My lectures are based primarily on Bruce Hansen's textbook.

Caveat: This is an ongoing writing project not ready to publish. The text is very incomplete, and must contain many errors. Use them at your own risk!
